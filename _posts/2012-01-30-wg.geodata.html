Working Group - Open Geospatial Data

Strapline:

Working Group for those interested in Open Geodata

Description:

Purpose Bring together representatives from existing groups for regular calls, to identify areas of potential collaboration, to undertake projects pertaining to geospatially referenced data Maintain registry of open geodata and geospatially referenced data on CKAN and improve CKAN's support for metadata for geospatial datasets.
