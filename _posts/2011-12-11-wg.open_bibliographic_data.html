Working Group - Open Bibliographic Data

Strapline:

Working Group for those interested in Open Bibliographic Data

Description:

Purpose Act as a central point of reference and support for people interested open bibliographic data Identify relevant projects and practices. Promote best practices as well as legal and technical standards for making data open (such as the Open Knowledge Definition).
