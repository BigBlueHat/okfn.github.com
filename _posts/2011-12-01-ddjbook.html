Strapline:

The How-To's of Data Journalism. Collective wisdom from journalists themselves.

Description:

The Data Journalism Handbook was drafted over 48 hours by 55 contributors including the The New York Times, the Guardian and OWNI. A comprehensive manual covering all aspects of data journalism, it will be released as a free e-book.
