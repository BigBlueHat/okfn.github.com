Working Group - Open Climate Science

Strapline:

Working Group for those interested

Description:

Purpose Act as a central point of reference and support for people who are interested in open climate data and software. Maintaining a registry of climate data sets such as descriptions, urls and licensing information. Encouraging best practices regarding licensing and publication of climate data. Act as a hub for the development of low cost, community driven projects around open climate data.
