Working Group - Open Visualisation Technologies

Strapline:

Working Group for those interested in Open Visualisation Technologies

Description:

Purpose Act as a central point of reference and support for people interested in open visualisation technologies. Organise open visualisation events Document existing visualisation packages and projects Act as a hub for the development of low cost, community driven open visualisation projects.
