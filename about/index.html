What is Labs?

We develop experimental tools and services that enable more people to use structured and semi-structured information on the web. Our technology helps to make government more accountable and participative, culture more accessible and science more efficient.

As part of the Open Knowledge Foundation Network we work as a collaborative community and we aim to use and produce open data and free software.
