What is Labs?

We're a home for experimental tools and services that enable more people to do useful stuff with data and content. We're interested in technology which helps to make government more accountable, culture more accessible and science more efficient.

We are part of the Open Knowledge Foundation. We operate as a collaborative community and use and produce open data and free and open source software.
